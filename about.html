About Us

A local, family-owned shop

Formats change. Memories don't. Past Forward is run by real people, right here in Indianapolis — not a mail-away chain.

Serving Indianapolis families

Every tape is inspected, cleaned, and digitized by hand — no outsourcing, no shortcuts.

Past Forward started the same way a lot of these tapes did — digging through a closet, wondering if anything on them still played. What began as saving our own family's home movies turned into a small local shop dedicated to doing right by everyone else's.

We're not a mail-away chain or a national franchise. Every tape and disc that comes through the door is inspected, cleaned, and digitized by hand, right here in Indianapolis — and every original always goes back to its owner.

  • We treat every tape like it's the only copy left.
  • We tell you upfront if something's at risk — no surprises.
  • You always get your original tapes and discs back.
