About Us

A local, family-owned shop

Past Forward is run by a college student, right here in Indianapolis.

Serving families around the Indianapolis area

Every tape is inspected, cleaned, and digitized by hand — no outsourcing, no shortcuts.

Hi! I'm Megan, the founder of Past Forward. For the past two years, I've had the privilege of helping families preserve their home videos and reconnect with memories they thought were tucked away forever. I love technology, but even more than that, I love using it to help people. There's nothing better than knowing someone gets to watch their childhood videos, hear the voices of loved ones again, or relive moments they haven't seen in decades.

I'm currently studying Computer Science at Purdue University, and Past Forward helps me work toward that goal while doing something I genuinely enjoy. Every tape and disc I convert is handled with the same care I'd want for my own family's memories. Thank you for trusting me to help preserve a piece of your story.
