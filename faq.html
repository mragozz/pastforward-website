FAQ

Common questions

Everything you might want to know before sending in your tapes.

Do I get my original tapes back? +
Yes, always. We only digitize — we never keep or discard your original tapes or discs.
What if a tape is damaged or won't play? +
We inspect every tape before running it. If something's damaged, we'll attempt a safe repair and let you know before we proceed — no surprise charges.
How do I get my tapes to you? +
Schedule a local pickup or drop-off in the Indianapolis area.
What format are the digital files? +
MP4 files organized by tape and date, playable on TVs, computers, and external drives delivered on a flash drive.
How long does it take? +
Most orders are ready in 1–2 weeks. Rush turnaround is available for an additional flat fee.

Still have questions?

We're happy to talk it through before you send anything in.
