The Process

How it works

Four steps, start to finish. We'll keep you posted along the way.

Drop off or mail in

Bring your tapes and discs to a scheduled local pickup, or mail them in with a padded box — we'll tell you how to pack them.

Inspect & clean

Every tape is inspected, cleaned, and repaired if needed before it ever touches a deck.

Digitize in real time

We capture each tape frame-by-frame on broadcast-quality decks — no shortcuts, no re-encoding twice.

Delivered to you

Files are organized by tape and date, loaded onto a flash drive or cloud download link, and your originals are returned.

About Us

A local, family-owned shop

Past Forward is run by real people, right here in Indianapolis — not a mail-away chain.

Serving Indianapolis families

Every tape is inspected, cleaned, and digitized by hand — no outsourcing, no shortcuts.

Past Forward started the same way a lot of these tapes did — digging through a closet, wondering if anything on them still played. What began as saving our own family's home movies turned into a small local shop dedicated to doing right by everyone else's.

We're not a mail-away chain or a national franchise. Every tape and disc that comes through the door is inspected, cleaned, and digitized by hand, right here in Indianapolis — and every original always goes back to its owner.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I get my original tapes back? +
Yes, always. We only digitize — we never keep or discard your original tapes or discs.
What if a tape is damaged or won't play? +
We inspect every tape before running it. If something's damaged, we'll attempt a safe repair and let you know before we proceed — no surprise charges.
How do I get my tapes to you? +
Schedule a local pickup or drop-off in the Indianapolis area.
What format are the digital files? +
MP4 files organized by tape and date, playable on TVs, computers, and external drives delivered on a flash drive.
How long does it take? +
Most orders are ready in 1–2 weeks. Rush turnaround is available for an additional flat fee.
