The Process

How it works

Four steps, start to finish. We'll keep you posted along the way.

Drop off or mail in

Bring your tapes and discs to a scheduled local pickup, or mail them in with a padded box — we'll tell you how to pack them.

Inspect & clean

Every tape is inspected, cleaned, and repaired if needed before it ever touches a deck.

Digitize in real time

We capture each tape frame-by-frame on broadcast-quality decks — no shortcuts, no re-encoding twice.

Delivered to you

Files are organized by tape and date, loaded onto a flash drive or cloud download link, and your originals are returned.
